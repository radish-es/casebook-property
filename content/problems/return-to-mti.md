For our problem this week, we will return to the Aboriginal Title claims being advanced by the Wolastoqey Nation and Mi’gmawe’l Tplu’taqnn Inc. (MTI) in Atlantic Canada.

Please review the pleadings in the these claims, which we first looked at in week 3. 

## The Problem

**How are the claimant Nations in the MTI pleadings approaching the question of whether or not Mi’gmaq Title is reconcilable with third-party fee simple interests? How is this approach similar to or different that those taken in the *Cowichan* and *J.D. Irving,v Wolastoqey Nation* decisions you read this week?**
