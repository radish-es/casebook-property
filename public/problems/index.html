    Problems

    Because this casebook is “problem-based”, each weekly problem provides the foundation on which to build your understanding of the different weekly readings and their relationship to one another (and to content you have studied in previous weeks).

    • 1. Intro Problem

      This is an introductory hypothetical problem.

    • 2. Wowkwis

      This hypothetical problem involves a dispute over legal entitlements to a wild animal pelt in the aftermath of fox hunt.

    • 3. The Wolastoqey Title Claim

      This problem addresses the ongoing title claim by the Wolastoqey Nation its traditional territory encompassing much of modern day New Brunswick, along with the Province's developing response to that claim.

    • 4. GMOs in West Egg

      This hypo concerns a market gardener's attempts to prevent pollination of their crops from GMO varieties grown by a neighbouring farmer.

    • 6. After the West Egg Protest

      Returning to the context of protests on West Egg's roads, this problem looks a hypothetical legislative changes through the lens of expropriation.

    • 7. Mid-Term Rapid Review

      A series of rapid review questions to assess your knowledge and understanding mid-term.

    • 8. Restraining Alienation

      West Egg's residents confront challenges around the legal tools available to restrain alienation and thereby influence the future of their community.

    • 9. A Letter to the Prime Minister

      A letter to Prime Minister WIlliam Lyon Mackenzie King serves at the starting point for addressing the history of Japanese-Canadian dispossession and internment.

    • 9. End-of-Term Rapid Review

      A series of rapid review questions to assess your knowledge and understanding at the end term.

    • 10. Flood on the Red River

      A flood in Winnipeg.

    • 11. Sheltering in Public Spaces Bylaw

      Municipal by-law regarding sheltering on public property.

    • 12. 3000 Funston Street

      Restrictive covenants in Houston.

    • 13. Mid-Term Rapid Review

      A series of rapid review questions to assess your knowledge and understanding at the end term.

    • 13. Shohei Ohtani's 50/50 Baseball

      Possession and baseball.

    • 15. Membertou's Transition to the First Nations Land Management Framework

      Membertou First Nation's adoption of the First Nations Land Management Framework

    • 16. Mi'gmawe'l Tplu'taqnn Inc. Statement of Claim

      Statement of claim re recent Aboriginal title litigation in New Brunswick

    • 17. End-of-Term Rapid Review

      A series of rapid review questions to assess your knowledge and understanding at the end of Winter term.
